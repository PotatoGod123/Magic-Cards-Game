Story of Majic The Gathering


In realm far across the galaxy, chaos consumed the planet Majicdor and humans and monsters were forced to battle for supreme control. Humans or Monsters need your help! Help your favorite faction by joining forces and conquering the opposition with this new exciting card game. Use your cards to attack and defend yourself! Be wary of the health bar, the fate of your faction depends on it. Achieve supreme control by depleting the oppositions health to 0. May your courage be met with the favors of the gods. Good luck!
